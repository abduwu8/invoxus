Invoxus — Privacy & Data Use Notice

Last updated:

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Invoxus accesses, uses, stores, shares, and deletes data, including Google user data, to deliver the product experience.

1. Data Access & Scope

  • With your consent, Invoxus accesses Gmail to list messages, read metadata and content for features like summaries, draft and send emails you approve, and help with unsubscribe or cleanup.
  • We request the minimum scopes required and use them only for the stated, user‑visible features.
  • We do not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.

2. Storage

  • Session credentials are stored in secure cookies to keep you signed in. Where you explicitly enable background actions, we may persist tokens in our database to execute the requested jobs.
  • Email content is not stored by default. Drafts or categories you explicitly save are stored to provide the feature.

3. Sharing

  • We do not share Google user data with third parties, except service providers strictly necessary to operate the service (e.g., infrastructure, background job processing) and bound by confidentiality.

4. Deletion & Revocation

  • You can revoke Invoxus access at any time from your Google Account security settings.
  • You may request deletion of stored tokens and app data by emailing contact@invoxus.email.

5. Security & Logs

  • We apply industry‑standard safeguards. Diagnostic logs, if used, exclude message content and PII.

6. Changes to this Policy

  • We will update this page if our data practices change and will notify users in‑product when material changes occur.

7. Contact

Questions or requests? Contact contact@invoxus.email.
